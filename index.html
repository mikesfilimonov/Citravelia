About Citravelia

Citravelia is an independent consultancy built around a single idea: that developers and private clients deserve the same rigor institutions bring to their real estate decisions, without the layers.

We work alongside our clients from the first site walk to the closing table — reading local markets, testing feasibility, and structuring deals that hold up under scrutiny. Our view spans Cyprus and the Globe, but our approach travels wherever the opportunity does.

Every mandate is handled directly, by people who know the file, not a rotating desk. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

How We Work

Three phases, one point of contact.

  • Discover

    Brief & Ground Truth

    We start by understanding the client's goals, constraints, and timeline, then verify the market and site data ourselves rather than taking listings at face value.

  • Evaluate

    Model & Stress-Test

    Every opportunity is run through feasibility and downside scenarios before it's brought to the client, so recommendations arrive already tested.

  • Execute

    Negotiate & Close

    We stay involved through negotiation, due diligence, and closing, coordinating with legal and financial partners so nothing is handed off blind.
